Answers to the most common questions about my services, process, pricing, and what it's like to work with me.

Getting Started

Simply head to the Contact page and send me a message describing your business and what you need. I'll get back to you within 1 business day to discuss your project, answer questions, and recommend the right package. There's no obligation — just a friendly conversation about your vision.

To get started I'll need your business name, preferred colors and fonts if you have them, any photos or content for the site, and a general idea of which pages you need. Don't worry if you don't have everything ready — I can help guide you through it all.

The Process

Delivery times depend on the package: Basic is 7 days, Standard is 14 days, and Premium is 21 days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee if you need it sooner. Timelines begin once I receive all your content and project details.

The Basic package includes 2 revisions, Standard includes 3, and Premium includes 5. Additional revisions are available for $15 each with a 1-day turnaround. I want you to be genuinely happy with the result, so revisions are always handled promptly.

Yes! Your website is built with clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files hosted on GitHub Pages. I'll hand off the full project with clear instructions so you or anyone with basic web knowledge can make updates. I'm also available for ongoing maintenance if you'd prefer to leave it in my hands.

Design & Technical

Every website I build is coded from scratch using clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No templates, no WordPress, no Wix or Squarespace. This means your site is completely unique, loads faster, and can be customized to exactly what you need without the limitations of a builder platform.

Absolutely. Every website I build is fully responsive and tested across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Most small business customers browse on their phones, so mobile-first design is a priority on every project — not an afterthought.

Yes — e-commerce and payment integration is one of my specialties. I build full shopping cart experiences with Stripe payment processing, supporting Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. The entire checkout flow is seamless and happens on one page. This is included in the Premium package or available as an add-on.

Hosting & Domain

I deploy all websites to GitHub Pages, which provides fast, reliable, and completely free hosting with no monthly fees. If your site has a backend (such as Stripe payment processing), the server is hosted on Render.com's free tier. Both are professional-grade platforms used by thousands of businesses.

No — hosting is completely free using GitHub Pages and Render.com. You will never pay a monthly hosting fee for a site I build. This is one of the biggest advantages of working with me over agencies or platforms like Wix and Squarespace that charge $15-$50 per month.

You don't need one to launch — your site can go live at a free GitHub Pages URL (e.g. yourbusiness.github.io). However a custom domain (e.g. yourbusiness.com) looks more professional and I can connect it to your site for an additional $50 setup fee. Domains typically cost $10-$15 per year from providers like GoDaddy or Namecheap.

Pricing & Payment

Payment is due after we confirm your project details and before I begin building. For larger Premium projects, I'm open to a 50% deposit upfront and 50% upon completion. We'll agree on the arrangement before any work begins.
