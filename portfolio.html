Project 01

Skylar's Plant
Based Cooking

Food & E-Commerce · Temecula, CA

E-Commerce Stripe Payments Node.js Backend Mobile Responsive 7 Pages

A complete e-commerce website for a Temecula-based plant-based food business. Built with a stunning cream and sage green design system featuring real food photography, a full shopping cart, and Stripe checkout supporting all major payment methods.

  • 7-page fully responsive website with elegant design
  • Slide-out shopping cart with item thumbnails
  • Stripe checkout — Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay & Google Pay
  • Node.js / Express backend hosted on Render.com
  • Order notification emails via Web3Forms
  • Delivery areas page with interactive Google Maps
  • FAQs accordion page and custom success page
  • Free hosting on GitHub Pages
